# Nyvora Vision Labs : **Open Source Computer vision Solutions**

---

## What We Do

We build and release open source tools for computer vision — from data collection and annotation pipelines to inference utilities and dataset schemas. Everything we build is free to use, fork, and contribute to.

- **Computer vision** — detection, classification, segmentation, and real-time inference pipelines
- **Data collection** — tools and scripts for sourcing and capturing visual data
- **Data annotation** — open utilities for labeling, reviewing, and exporting training datasets

We are not a company or a service provider. We are a lab — a group building in the open because we believe better tooling should be accessible to everyone, not locked behind enterprise contracts.

---

## Our Philosophy

Most computer vision tooling is either fragmented, paywalled, or buried inside closed platforms. We think the community deserves solid, well-documented, composable tools that just work.

Everything here is open source. Use it in your research, your projects, your products. If something is broken or missing, open an issue or send a PR.
